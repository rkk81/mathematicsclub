Basic Probability

A bag contains 3 red marbles, 4 blue marbles, and 5 green marbles. What is the probability of drawing a blue marble?

Let's solve step by step:

1. Total number of marbles = 3 + 4 + 5 = 12

2. Number of favorable outcomes (blue marbles) = 4

3. Probability = Favorable outcomes/Total outcomes

4. = 4/12 = 1/3

Answer: The probability is 1/3
